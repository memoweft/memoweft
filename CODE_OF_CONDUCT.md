# Code of Conduct

MemoWeft is committed to a professional, respectful, and technically constructive community.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and assume good intent while evaluating ideas on their merits.
- Give actionable feedback and explain technical trade-offs.
- Welcome people with different backgrounds and experience levels.
- Respect privacy. Never post credentials, private memory databases, or personal user data.
- Keep project spaces focused on the work and its users.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, deliberate disruption, doxxing, sexualized conduct, and publication of another person's private information are not acceptable in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, commits, issues, pull requests, discussions, or other contributions that violate this policy. Repeated or serious violations may result in a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

Report conduct concerns through the repository's private security-report form and prefix the title with `[Conduct]`. Include links or screenshots and enough context to assess the report. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical, with attention to the safety of the reporter and affected participants.

This policy applies to the repository, issue tracker, pull requests, discussions, and other official MemoWeft community spaces.
